# 3. Hyperbolic Tangent


```python
A = np.random.rand(3,5)
x = np.random.rand(5,1)
b = np.random.rand(3,1)

print('A =\n', A)
print('x =\n', x)
print('b =\n', b)

y = np.tanh(np.dot(A,x) + b)
print('y =\n', y)

del A, x, b, y
```
